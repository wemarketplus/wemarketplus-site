⚙️ Automation

Automatic follow-up actions tied to specific prospects and referral sources

📌 How this works: Select a prospect, then trigger an action. In the live CRM, these actions automatically send reminders, schedule follow-ups, and alert you when a prospect goes cold. In this demo, clicking a button shows you a logged confirmation of what would happen.

Step 1 — Select a Prospect or Referral Source

Select a prospect above

Step 2 — Choose an Automation Action

Each action fires once for the selected prospect and logs a timestamped record.

🔁 Auto Follow-Up
Schedules a follow-up reminder for this prospect 3 days from today. You'll see it in your Reminders tab.
📞 Touch Cadence
Sets a recurring weekly check-in reminder for this prospect's referral source. Keeps the relationship warm.
📄 Request Paperwork
Sends an alert to your team that paperwork (H&P, insurance) is needed for this prospect to move forward.
⚠️ Overdue Alert
Flags this prospect as overdue and sends you an alert. Appears in your Reminders tab as urgent.
🎉 Admission Ready
Marks this prospect as Pending Admission and notifies your clinical team that they're ready for intake.
💌 Family Outreach
Triggers a reminder to reach out to the family within 24 hours. Appears in your Reminders tab as due today.

🗄️ Data Retention Policy

Marketing leads: Archived after 36 months, restorable anytime.
Mileage / GPS logs: Archived after 48 months (IRS compliance).
Clinical notes, family communications, HIPAA logs: Retained 6–10 years per federal HIPAA requirements. Never auto-archived. Cannot be deleted by users.
