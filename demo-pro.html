HospiceLink Pro Guide
What each tab does
Dashboard
CRM overview — pipeline, referral stats, and activity at a glance.
Add Prospect
Add a new patient lead. Fill in contact info, status, referral source, and next step.
Prospect Records
Full prospect table. Use 📱 Text and 📧 Email buttons — auto-logs to Notes.
Referral Sources
All referral partners. Green = active. Red = not sending. Text or email directly.
Referral Records
Searchable log of all referral activity.
Pipeline
Visual funnel: Inquiry → Pending Admission → Admitted → Lost.
Territory View
Map view of your coverage area.
Productivity
Track marketer visits, calls, and follow-up activity.
Automation
Set up automatic follow-up reminders and alerts.
Notes
Field notes with GPS sync. All SMS/email communications auto-log here.
🤖 AI Assistant
Ask Claude AI for scripts, analysis, strategies, and reports.
🔒 Admin
Edit/delete any record. Manage staff accounts. Admin only.
📱 SMS & Email Auto-Logging
On any Prospect or Referral Source record, click 📱 Text or 📧 Email.

1. Type your message in the popup
2. Your phone SMS or email app opens pre-filled
3. Send from your device
4. Auto-logged to Notes with date, time, contact and message.

No manual logging ever needed.
